> **Abstract:** The pinball loss is favored in support vector machines for its robustness against noise. However, its unbounded nature makes it sensitive to outliers. To address this, a rescaled pinball loss offering boundedness has been introduced. This loss is characterized by properties such as asymmetry, non-convexity, sparsity, boundedness, and differentiability. It is applied to support vector machines and is referred to as SRGP-SVM.
